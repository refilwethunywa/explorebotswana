Travel Information & Tips

Planning a trip to Botswana? Here are essential travel details to help you prepare for a safe and unforgettable safari experience.

  • Best time to visit: May – October (dry season, best wildlife viewing)
  • Currency: Botswana Pula (BWP)
  • Language: English (official), Setswana widely spoken
  • Etiquette: Respect local customs and wildlife conservation rules
