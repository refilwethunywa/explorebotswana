Wildlife & National Parks

Botswana is home to some of Africa’s most spectacular wildlife. Visitors can explore rich biodiversity across its famous national parks, each offering unique landscapes and safari experiences.

National Parks Overview

National Park / Reserve Highlights
Chobe National Park Large elephant herds, river safaris, diverse wildlife viewing
Okavango Delta Wetlands, mokoro canoe rides, bird watching, water safaris
Moremi Game Reserve Big cats, antelopes, rich biodiversity, classic safari experiences
